Welcome to Sparkly Wordle!

How to Play!

Guess the WORDLE in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples

The letter W is in the word and in the correct spot. (Highlighted Sparkly Purple)

The letter I is in the word but in the wrong spot. (Highlighted Pale Purple)

The letter U is not in the word in any spot. (Highlighted Grey)
